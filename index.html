More about ConnectSA

ConnectSA was founded by Ruan Hattingh and Dean Zvauya, two passionate individuals who recognized the challenges South Africans face in accessing essential services. They wanted to create a platform that simplifies the process of finding reliable support, ensuring that everyone can get the help they need without unnecessary delays or confusion.

We believe that access to essential services is a fundamental right, and our mission is to bridge the gap between those in need and the organizations that can provide assistance. By leveraging technology and community engagement, ConnectSA aims to empower individuals and strengthen communities across South Africa.

Founded by Ruan Hattingh and Dean Zvauya, ConnectSA is dedicated to helping South Africans access essential services quickly and easily.
